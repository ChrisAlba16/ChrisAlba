Motivation & passion

Since I was a kid, I had such a big passion for breaking down anything

in front of me, just to know what is inside. Puzzles, riddles, and big

questions have always flooded my mind. “I wanna be an inventor”

I used to say, yes, “used to” because now, I am.

Bio

I was born on March 28th in Chiapas, Mexico. From my early days I  remember loving to run and swim all day, I’ve always been quite an  energetic person with such an enormous curiosity for the phenomena of the world. As a suburban kid I did not have a lot of friends, but  instead, I had the biggest playground a kid had ever imagine. When I was in high school, I used to ramble about studying physics until I got to calculus, and I discover that the beauty I saw in physics was in fact math in disguise, but the real breakthrough didn’t hit me until I first hear about NP problems. A totally new world was opened in front of my eyes, the possibility of solving problems for living was my ideal. That is why I decide to study computer science, Nowadays I live in Campeche, Mexico, and I study at the Autonomous University of Campeche, slowly but  constantly preparing so I can achieve my dream of discover the mechanism of the universe itself.

Christopher Alba Arcos - 2021 - UAC
